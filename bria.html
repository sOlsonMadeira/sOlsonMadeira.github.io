Backstory

The first pet I have ever had was my dog Bria. She is a Golden Doodle from North Carolina. When I was four years old we drove up to North Carolina to find a big box of puppies. They told us to pick one, and I'm pretty sure it was my sister Olivia who picked out Bria. We walked in with many name Ideas, but upon choosing her they already had a name for her (Bria), so we went with it. She is now 11 years old.
