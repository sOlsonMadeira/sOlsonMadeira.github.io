Halley!

Backstory

I had wanted a dog since I was little, so last year I decided to make a slideshow to convince my dad to adopt a dog. It ended up working and we were planning on adopting a dog in April, after spring break. When corona started and we had to cancel our spring break, we pushed up our timeline to get a dog,and we found Halley! On March 21st, 2020, we drove to Pennsylvania to go get her!

Characteristics
